A margin is the outerspace around a border. A margin is transparent, and is used to format a document, separate boxes from other boxes.

A border surrounds the perimeter of the padding and the content, and can vary in size, shape, and thickness

Padding provides a buffer space between the content and border of a webpage.

Content

The CSS Block Model is a tool used to organize HTML elements such as the division "div", paragraph "p", and "section" tags.

The CSS Block Model can be organized into 4 categories Content, Padding, Border, and Margin.

Of these categories Content is the most important, it is the reason for the visit to the webpage.

However that does not mean the other categories are not important. They play an important role in delivering content to the web browser and viewer.
